What is the cobb douglas production calculator?
The Cobb-Douglas production function calculator is a tool that measures the total production of a product according to the Cobb-Douglas production function.
How to calculate cobb douglas production?
To calculate cobb douglas production you should use the following formula:
total production = total factor productivity * labor^output elasticity of labor * capital^output elasticity of capital
